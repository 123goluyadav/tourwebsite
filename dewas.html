Dewas is a city of Madhya Pradesh. Dewas is located near to Indore city. Dewas is about 150 km away from Bhopal. Bhopal is the capital of Madhya Pradesh. Dewas city is situated on Indore and Bhopal highway road. You can come here comfortably. Dewas city is famous for its many specialties. There is Tekri temple in Dewas city which is famous all over India. Here, a temple of Mata is built in a high Tekri. There is a note printing factory in Dewas. Dewas is a small city but there has a lots of tourist spot. you can visit Dewas and roam. Shipra river is flows in dewas city. Shipra river is a main river in the Dewas city. There is a dam built on Shipra river near Dewas city which is very beautiful. There are many places to visit in Dewas city where you can spend your time. Chamunda Mata Temple Dewas

Chamunda Mata Temple and Tulja Bhavani Temple is a famous place in all madhya pradesh. This is a religious place in Dewas. This place is also known as Dewas Tekri or Chamunda Tekri. This temple is located in main city. Chamunda temple is situated on a high hill. You can easily go to the temple by road or stairs. Ropeway facility is available to reach the temple.

Pushpagiri Teerth is a major tourist destination in Dewas. This is a Jain religious place. This jain temple is located in Pushpagiri town. Pushpagiri Jain Tirth is about 20 kms from Dewas.
